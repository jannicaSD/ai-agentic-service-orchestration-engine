## **Technical Notes for Video Production**

### **Animations & Motion Graphics:**
1. **Agent pipeline**: Show agents running in parallel (not sequential) with animated progress bars
2. **Ranking algorithm**: Real-time calculation with factors animating in:
   - Rating: green bar fills +0.441
   - Distance: yellow bar fills +0.261
   - Experience: blue bar fills +0.173
   - Total score animates to 0.887
3. **GPS tracking**: Live map showing provider approach with ETA countdown
4. **State machine**: Vertical stepper with smooth transitions (✅ → 🔄 → ✅)
5. **Trace logs**: Animated scroll through decision audit trail with expandable sections

### **Key UX Elements to Feature:**
- Chat bubbles (user → assistant)
- ProviderCard scroll (horizontal)
- BookingTimelineScreen (vertical stepper)
- TraceLogsScreen (expandable tree)
- Provider profile photos & ratings

### **Talking Points for Script:**
- **Agency**: "Antigravity makes decisions, not users. This creates trust."
- **Fairness**: "11 factors prevent gaming the system — no single metric dominates."
- **Transparency**: "Every decision is traced and visible. No black box."
- **Auditability**: "Perfect for regulatory compliance and mediating disputes."
- **Multilingual**: "Speaks Urdu, English, and Roman Urdu — no language barriers."
